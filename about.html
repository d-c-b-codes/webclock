welcome to webclock, a project i made to practice js logic, js time, js random ints, manipulating objects into & out of json, and dealing with local storage.


webclock allows you to clock in & out, keeping track of the amount of time you spend on different projects.


webclock uses your local storage to keep your font & color settings, as well as the individual time-in/out entries that you create. you can clear your local storage at any time using the button at the bottom of the home page.
